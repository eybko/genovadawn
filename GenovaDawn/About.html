About

We are a state of the art equestrian training and boarding facility. Located outside of Nashville, Tennessee, we offer beautiful views nestled within the surrounding rolling hills. We were founded in 2002 by our CEO and owner, Faith Ralph. Having immense passion for animals her whole life, she strove to create a place that all could come to regardless of age and experience level. Faith believes that creating a life long bond with a horse is an experience everyone should be able to have in their lifetimes.

To ensure that this goal was attainable, Faith built Genova Dawn from the ground up on 100 acres of land. Hiring top trainers from the surrounding area, our training program is one of the top in the country. We also provide top boarding facilities for those who wish to pleasure ride or simply spend time with their horses. We aim to accomdate both riders and their companions through our ethics and facilities.
